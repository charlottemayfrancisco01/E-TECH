E-TECH

INTRODUCTION

E-Tech is a web-based platform that uses interactive storytelling to make learning fun and engaging for Grade 4 learners. It combines stories with interactive activities to enhance reading comprehension, creativity, and basic ICT skills. Aligned with the DepEd MATATAG curriculum, E-Tech helps students learn meaningfully while developing digital literacy.


WHAT WE DO

E-Tech is a learner-centered web-based platform designed to support the Department of Education’s MATATAG curriculum. We focus on empowering Grade 4 learners by promoting literacy, digital competence, creativity, and critical thinking through interactive storytelling and technology-enhanced learning experiences.


HOW WE DO IT

We develop web-based interactive storytelling materials that are carefully aligned with DepEd Grade 4 learning competencies. Our resources are crafted to strengthen reading comprehension, communication skills, creativity, and basic ICT skills. By ensuring that every material is engaging, age-appropriate, and inclusive, we provide digital learning experiences that encourage meaningful exploration and independent learning, helping young learners grow with confidence in both academics and technology.
